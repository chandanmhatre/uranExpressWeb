Focus

Foc’us….the very name suggests of a place that is much more than just a gymnasium. Conceptualized from the single-minded focus that enabled the proprietor and the renowned international athlete Mr. Prashant Patil to become the best he could be, Fo’cus is driven with the idea of bringing the best out of its clients. Foc’us understands fully well that only when the body and mind are of sound health, and working in unison, a human being can keep reaching new heights of achievement, whether as a sportsman, or in any other walk of life.

Mission

Train bodies and minds to reach the epitome of their health in order to create the best possible versions of human beings.

Becoming a household name in the field of body-mind fitness through promoting sports, encouraging participation, and helping people reach their peak performance as professionals or amateurs.
